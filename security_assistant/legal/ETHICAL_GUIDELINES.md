### 1. Authorization First
- **Always** obtain written permission before testing
- **Never** test systems you don't own or control
- **Document** all authorizations

### 2. Minimize Impact
- Use read-only modes when possible
- Avoid disruptive scans during business hours
- Test in staging environments first

### 3. Transparency
- Disclose your testing activities
- Provide clear reports to system owners
- Document all findings and actions

### 4. Professionalism
- Follow industry standards (OWASP, NIST)
- Respect system owners' time and resources
- Maintain confidentiality

### 5. Continuous Learning
- Stay updated on security best practices
- Share knowledge responsibly
- Improve your skills ethically
